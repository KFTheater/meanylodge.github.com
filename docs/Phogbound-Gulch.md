### Location

The long narrow gully on the south side of South Slobbovia.<sup>[nw][]</sup>

### Origin

Named in honor of Senator Jack S. Phogbound, Lower Slobbovia's representative to the Senate of the Hew Hess Hay.<sup>[nw][]</sup>
